Groupwork VS Indiviual Work


Overall, I was happy with my teammate. We could have done more together as a team, and tended to let the other teammate take the whole assignment and take turns doing that. That would lead to me being a bit confused on the next assignment when he had done the previous one, and it would take 30ish minutes to understand what the previous code meant. It was very nice to have that collaboration with the teams, and I generally prefered it to working alone. I did feel like I was able to buckle down and get more done on the tests and final exam when I was working alone, but I don't think I would have liked it for the whole course to be like that.


How were the Tests and Workload?


I thought the tests were rather difficult, but I still felt like we had learned all the techniques we needed for them. They generally reqiured me analytical thought and brain power than the homework assignments did. The workload felt like a good sweetspot. I was able to continually work on this course so I would be using what I learned, but I didn't totally speed through it. I feel like there was a good amount of time between when the assignments were due.
